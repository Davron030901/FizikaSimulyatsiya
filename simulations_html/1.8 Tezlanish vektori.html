📚 Nazariy ma'lumotlar

Asosiy formulalar:
$$\vec{a} = \frac{d\vec{v}}{dt} = \frac{\Delta\vec{v}}{\Delta t}$$ $$a_n = \frac{v^2}{r}$$ (markazga intiluvchi) $$a_t = \frac{dv}{dt}$$ (tangensial) $$|\vec{a}| = \sqrt{a_n^2 + a_t^2}$$
