Elastik to'qnashish - bu jismlarning to'qnashishida kinetik energiya saqlanib qoladigan to'qnashish turi.

❓
Impuls saqlanish qonuni:
$$m_1v_1 + m_2v_2 = m_1v_1' + m_2v_2'$$
❓
Energiya saqlanish qonuni:
$$\frac{1}{2}m_1v_1^2 + \frac{1}{2}m_2v_2^2 = \frac{1}{2}m_1v_1'^2 + \frac{1}{2}m_2v_2'^2$$
❓
Restitution koeffitsienti (e = 1):
$$e = \frac{v_2' - v_1'}{v_1 - v_2} = 1$$
